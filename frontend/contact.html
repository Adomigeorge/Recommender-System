Frequently Asked Questions

How does the recommendation system work?
Our system uses content-based filtering with cosine similarity. It analyzes movie features (genres, cast, keywords, etc.) to find movies similar to your input.
Do I need to create an account?
No! You can get recommendations without signing up. Accounts are optional for saving preferences.
How many movies are in your database?
We currently have thousands of movies in our database, with new ones added regularly.
Can I suggest a movie to add?
Absolutely! Use the contact form above to suggest movies you'd like to see in our database.
